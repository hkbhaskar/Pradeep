  • HOTELS

    • HOTEL PRADEEP, Varanasi

      Established in 1980, Hotel Pradeep, is a 3 Star hotel approved by Ministry of Tourism, Govt. of India and is the flag bearer of the firm. The majestic building stands tall in the heart of the eternal city of Varanasi, offering all the luxuries of a world class hotel. The Hotel is appreciated by both domestic as well as foreign tourists and is often highly recommended in the International Travelers Guide Books for India like India Survival Kit, Lonely Planet, Retard ( French Guide Book of India ) etc.

    • PALACE ON GANGES, Varanasi

      Palace on Ganges is a grand heritage boutique hotel situated on the famous Assi Ghat, on the banks of the holy river Ganges in Varanasi. It is a unique property where colours & culture of india are showcased together, all under one roof. Each of its 24 rooms represents a different culture and theme and offers a one of a kind experience. No wonder it is the preferred choice of foreign tourists & quality domestic travellers.

    • Palash Kothi, Bandhavgarh(MP)

      Resort Palash Kothi is a quaint resort set in the pleasant and lush green world adjacent to the Bandhavgarh Tiger Reserve. The Resort is appreciated by both domestic as well as foreign tourists and is often highly recommended in the International Travelers Guide Books for India like India Survival Kit, Lonely Planet, Retard ( French Guide Book of India ) etc.
