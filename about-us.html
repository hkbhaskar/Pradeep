ABOUT US

A Few Words About Us

INTRODUCTION

What We Do

Established in the year 1980, Hotel Pradeep is a dynamic organisation in the hospitality sector that endeavours to provide its guests with the highest standards of service, care and a high quality experience. The activities of the firm include Hotels (since 1980), In-flight Catering Services and Food & Beverage Outlets (since 1994). It carries out operations through its head office at Jagatganj, Varanasi and various units / branches at Varanasi Airport, Lucknow Airport, Guwahati Airport, Bagdogra Airport and Ranchi Airport.

Hotel Pradeep is ISO 22000:2005 / ISO 9001:2015 certified and HACCP & HALAL compliant.

MISSION

To provide our clients and customers with hospitality and catering services of the highest quality and value in a professional, friendly and responsive manner consistent with our goal of “Attaining Perfection”.

OUR SERVICES

What We Offer

Hotel Pradeep is presently operating various niche bistros, lounges and food & beverage outlets at various airports across the country. The various brands set up and operated by the firm include Food Hangar (Varanasi Airport), Bakes & Beans, Zesto (Varanasi, Bagdogra, Ranchi airports) , The Coastal Café, Masala Anna and the airport executive lounge called Take Off Bar at Varanasi Airport. The firm has been awarded master concession to Develop, Market, Setting up, Operating, Maintaining and Managing the Food & Beverages Outlets for the entire Guwahati Airport. The work is under execution and will be operational soon.
